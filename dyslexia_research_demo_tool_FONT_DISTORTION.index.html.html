Dyslexia Transposed-Letter Research/Demo Tool

Workshop tool for demonstrating text disruption effects and capturing participant reading time, comprehension, confidence, perceived effort and NASA-TLX-style workload ratings.

Important: This is not a diagnostic tool and does not claim to reproduce dyslexia accurately. It demonstrates selected barriers such as transposed letters, confusable letters, timing pressure, visual jitter, visual distortion/crowding and font presentation for discussion and inclusive design teaching.
